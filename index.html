MauriServices is a web and mobile-friendly platform designed to digitize local services in Mauritania. It allows users to order food from nearby restaurants and book appointments with various local professionals (doctors, dentists, barbers, garages, etc.) via a unified, accessible interface.

Modules

  • MauriEats: Search for restaurants, view menus, and place orders.
  • MauriBeauty: Book appointments with local beauty professionals (hairdressers, barbers, spa etc.).
  • MauriHealth: Book appointments with local health professionals (doctors, dentists, etc.).
  • MauriServices: Book appointments with local service providers (plumbers, electricians, etc.).

Available Modules

  • Eats - Food delivery
  • Beauty - Wellness & Spa
  • Sales - Retail & Offers
